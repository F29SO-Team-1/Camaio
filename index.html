Camaio

Provided service from Camaio

The type of services that Camaio is proud to present is a new and totally not saturated market of social medias. The service will be very strict on the type of posts that the user can post. This is what will make us unique it will be a non-human social media. You might think how that will work. Well. It will only be able to post pictures and a small description of the post, with the unique feature of have no human parts on the website. With our AI we can detect any human elements that are in the picture ranging from a foot to a full body the AI will just no allow the user to post it.
